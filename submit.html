What we do with your answers

Used in: the public "How We Invest" guide and benchmarks and social content, and anonymous datasets we may share with research partners (fintechs, journalists, regulators).

Never: sold or shared with your name attached. Your personal information (name and email) is for our internal verification only and is never published.

Your rights: you can ask us to delete your submission at any time by emailing alice@gfy.co.uk.
