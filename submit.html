What you're doing

This guide was built from 500+ anonymous Instagram Story responses from the GFY community. We're keeping it open — anyone who reads the guide can also help build it. Your answers feed the next edition, the live community quotes on every page, and the research we publish each year.

~3 minutes · 18 short questions, mostly multiple choice
Anonymous · No name, no contact, no tracking
Optional · Every free-text question is skippable

What we do with your answers

Used in: the public "How We Invest" guide and benchmarks, our annual research report, GFY's newsletter and social content, and anonymous datasets we may share with research partners (fintechs, journalists, regulators).

Never: sold or shared with your name attached. Your Instagram handle (if you share it) is for our internal verification only and is never published.

Your rights: you can ask us to delete your submission at any time by emailing hello@gofundyourself.co.

About this project. "How We Invest" is a community-built guide from @gofundyourself. Every number, chart and quote on this page comes from anonymous Instagram Story responses collected in March 2026 and ongoing community submissions through this form.

Affiliate links. Some platforms mentioned in the guide have affiliate programmes. Where a platform has been linked, GFY may receive a small commission if you sign up. This has no influence on the data: the guide reports what the community said, not what anyone is promoting.

Not financial advice. Nothing in this guide is a recommendation to buy, sell, or invest in anything specific. Investing carries risk. The value of your investments can fall as well as rise. Only invest what you can afford to leave alone.
