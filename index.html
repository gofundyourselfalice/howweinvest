Why this exists

Investing, by which we mean building your wealth over the long term, has historically been the preserve of finance experts and men in suits. This is changing, but there are still lots of people in the UK who could be making the most of this opportunity. Perhaps one of them is you.

This project is a window into how normal people actually invest, and what it has enabled them to do.
