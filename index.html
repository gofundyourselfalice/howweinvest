Is this just for women?

No, not explicitly. Investing is for everyone. But GFY, the community behind this project, is mostly (though not only) women. It's also often women who feel most excluded from investing, so that's where our heart is, but we warmly welcome and encourage everyone to contribute, whoever you are.

Why the cat?

The imagery around investing is unhelpful: charts, bulls, "buy, sell, buy, sell!". It all suggests frantic action. Good investing tends to be the opposite; the less you do, the better you usually do. Much like a sleepy cat, it's really about staying calm, leaving things be, and getting on with enjoying your life.
