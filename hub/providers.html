Choose your platform

The platforms the GFY community use most, listed alphabetically — no single "winner," just what real investors actually do.

This list comes from the same crowdsourced project as the rest of the guide. We asked 500+ predominantly UK-based women which platforms they actually use to invest — what they've signed up for, stuck with, and would recommend to a friend.

Below are the most common picks, grouped by what kind of platform each one is and listed alphabetically. No leaderboard, no "best buy." Pick the one that fits how hands-on you want to be, your monthly amount, and your patience for an app vs. a desktop interface.

Where a platform has been linked, GFY may receive a small commission if you sign up. This has no influence on what's listed — every platform here came from community responses, before any affiliate conversation started.
