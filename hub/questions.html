Community questions

Got a question that isn't answered in the guide? Ask it here. Answered by the GFY team within 48 hours, and by other readers in between.

Coming soon Community forum. Questions submitted by anyone, answered by the GFY team and/or upvoted and answered by other community members. Organised by category matching the guide chapters. Most-asked questions surface to the top. Moderated. New questions flagged for the team to answer within 48 hours.

Categories the forum will cover

  • Getting started — first steps, opening an ISA, choosing a starting platform
  • Choosing investments — which fund, single fund vs portfolio, ESG considerations
  • How much & how often — monthly amounts, life changes, lump sums
  • Platforms — switching, fees, app vs desktop
  • When things wobble — market dips, what to do, what not to do
  • Beyond the basics — SIPPs, rebalancing, going over the ISA allowance
  • Specific situations — investing for kids, self-employed, near-retirement, expats
Also coming "Was this helpful?" thumbs up/down on every guide section, feeding back into which sections to improve or expand.
