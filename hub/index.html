About this project. "How We Invest" is a community-built guide from @gofundyourself. Every number, chart and quote comes from anonymous Instagram Story responses collected in March 2026.

Affiliate links. Some platforms in the provider hub have affiliate programmes. Where a platform has been linked, GFY may receive a small commission if you sign up. This has no influence on the data: the guide reports what the community said, not what anyone is promoting.

Not financial advice. The value of your investments can fall as well as rise, and you may get back less than you put in. Only invest what you can afford to leave alone.
