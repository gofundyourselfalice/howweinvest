The full disclaimers

Everything we want you to know about who we are and the limits of what we're allowed to say.

About this project

"How We Invest" is a community-built guide from Go Fund Yourself (GFY). Every number, chart and quote in this guide comes from survey responses submitted to GFY from March 2026 onwards. We're starting with responses from 500+ people, but are continuing to collect the data via the share-your-story form.

This guide is a snapshot of what 500+ real people in the UK said they do with their money. It is not a recommendation, and it is not financial advice.

Methodology, and how to read the data

The data comes from an open survey shared with the GFY community and anyone who finds this site, collected from March 2026 onwards and still growing. Everything is self-reported. The first wave is 500+ responses, and the live count updates as more come in.

This is a self-selecting community sample, not a nationally representative survey. Respondents are predominantly UK-based women who already follow GFY, or who cared enough to answer, so they tend to be more engaged with money than the average person. We never claim these figures represent the whole UK population. We say "of our community" or "of respondents" on purpose, and that's how they should be read.

Free-text answers, things like goals, mistakes and what investing enabled, are read and grouped into themes by hand. Percentages for those are the share of respondents who named each theme, and many people named more than one. Numbers are rounded. Where a figure draws on a source beyond our own survey, it's linked at the point we use it. If you spot something that looks off, please tell us and we'll fix it.

Not financial advice

Nothing in this guide is a recommendation to buy, sell, or invest in anything specific. Every person's financial situation is different. If you want personalised advice, speak to a qualified financial advisor.

Investing can help your money work harder, but it comes with risk. The value of your investments can fall as well as rise, and you may get back less than you put in. Only invest what you can afford to leave alone.

Historical returns shown in this guide (the inflation visualiser, the compounding calculator, etc.) are illustrative, past performance does not guarantee future results.

Links to platforms

After creating this guide, Alice @ GFY reached out to the top 10 providers mentioned by the community, asking if they'd like to add a sign-up offer and a comment of their own.

Some of the links to platforms are affiliate links, which means GFY may earn a small commission if you sign up through them. Where a link is an affiliate link, it's labelled as such. Either way it has no influence on the data or the order here: the list came from community responses first, before any conversation with a platform.

Data collection & privacy

The dataset behind this guide is anonymous. We never publish names, handles, or any identifying information without explicit permission. If you submit a story via the contribute form, you can ask us to delete it at any time by emailing alice@gfy.co.uk.

Cookies

This site doesn't set tracking cookies. We don't run ads. We don't have analytics that follow you around the internet.
