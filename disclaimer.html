About this project

"How We Invest" is a community-built guide from @gofundyourself (GFY). Every number, chart and quote in this guide comes from anonymous Instagram Story responses collected in March 2026, supplemented by responses that come in via the share-your-story form.

This guide is a snapshot of what 500+ real people in the UK said they do with their money. It is not a recommendation, and it is not financial advice.

Not financial advice

Nothing in this guide is a recommendation to buy, sell, or invest in anything specific. Every person's financial situation is different. If you want personalised advice, speak to a qualified financial advisor.

Investing can help your money work harder, but it comes with risk. The value of your investments can fall as well as rise, and you may get back less than you put in. Only invest what you can afford to leave alone.

Historical returns shown in this guide (the inflation visualiser, the compounding calculator, etc.) are illustrative — past performance does not guarantee future results.

Affiliate links

After creating this guide, the GFY team reached out to the top 10 providers mentioned by the community asking if they would like to provide a sign-up offer and an affiliate link.

Where a platform has been linked, GFY may receive a small commission if you sign up. This has no influence on the data: the guide reports what the community said, not what anyone is promoting. Provider rankings and stats came from community responses before the affiliate conversations started.

All affiliate links are marked with a "Capital at risk · Affiliate link" tag.

Data collection & privacy

The dataset behind this guide is anonymous. We never publish names, handles, or any identifying information without explicit permission. If you submit a story via the contribute form, you can ask us to delete it at any time by emailing hello@gofundyourself.co.

Cookies

This site doesn't set tracking cookies. We don't run ads. We don't have analytics that follow you around the internet.
