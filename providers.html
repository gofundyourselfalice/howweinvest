About this project. "How We Invest" is a community-built guide from @gofundyourself. Every number, chart and quote on this page comes from anonymous Instagram Story responses collected in March 2026.

Affiliate links. After creating this guide, the GFY team reached out to the top 10 providers mentioned asking if they would like to provide a sign-up offer and affiliate link. Some platforms have affiliate programmes. Where a platform has been linked, GFY may receive a small commission if you sign up. This has no influence on the data.

Not financial advice. Nothing on this page is a recommendation to buy, sell, or invest in anything specific. It is a snapshot of what real people said they do with their money. Investing can help your money work harder, but it comes with risk. The value of your investments can fall as well as rise, and you may get back less than you put in. Only invest what you can afford to leave alone.
